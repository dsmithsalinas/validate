## Brand Summary

Validate is an AI-powered product investment reasoning engine.

The brand should feel:

- clear
- rigorous
- modern
- trustworthy
- evidence-driven
- minimal
- confident without feeling flashy

Validate is **not** a generic AI assistant brand.
It should not feel playful, noisy, or gimmicky.

---

## Core Brand Idea

**Evidence becomes decision.**

The selected logo direction represents multiple evidence inputs converging into a clear decision point.

This concept is the core of the Validate identity.

---

## Logo Direction

### Primary Mark

Use the selected **evidence-to-decision** symbol:

- layered horizontal shapes converging into a right-facing arrow
- visually represents evidence flowing toward a decision
- clean geometric structure
- minimal silhouette
- works in monochrome

### Primary Wordmark Lockup

Preferred lockup:

- icon on the left
- "Validate" wordmark on the right
- strong spacing
- clean sans-serif feel
- black on white by default

### Secondary Lockups

Approved secondary uses:

1. Stacked lockup
2. Compact horizontal lockup
3. Circular app badge / badge lockup
4. Symbol-only icon

---

## Color System

Validate should remain **black and white** for now.

### Primary Colors

- Black: `#000000`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

### Supporting Neutrals

- Near Black: `#111111`
- Dark Gray: `#333333`
- Mid Gray: `#777777`
- Light Gray: `#D9D9D9`
- Very Light Gray: `#F5F5F5`

### Usage Guidance

- Default background: white
- Default logo: black
- Reversed logo: white on black
- Grayscale may be used in presentations, guidelines, and mockups
- Do not introduce gradients or bright accent colors at this stage

---

## Typography Direction

Typography should feel neutral, precise, and modern.

Recommended characteristics:

- clean sans-serif
- highly legible
- minimal styling
- not overly rounded
- not overly decorative

Suggested families to test:

- Inter
- Geist
- Suisse Int'l style
- Helvetica Now style
- SF Pro style

### Typography Roles

#### Heading Style

- bold or semibold
- strong clarity
- spacious, modern layout

#### Body Style

- regular weight
- highly readable
- restrained and functional

## Brand Tone

Validate should feel like:

- a trusted product advisor
- a decision system
- a rigorous internal tool
- a premium, modern software brand

It should not feel like:

- a chatbot
- a consumer AI toy
- a consulting firm
- a playful productivity app

---

## Logo Usage Rules

### Do

- use the evidence-to-decision mark consistently
- preserve spacing around the logo
- use monochrome by default
- use the horizontal lockup as the primary brand expression
- use symbol-only versions for small surfaces like favicons and app icons

### Don't

- stretch or distort the mark
- rotate the mark
- add gradients
- add shadows or effects
- change the geometry casually
- swap in unrelated icons
- crowd the mark with text or containers unnecessarily

---

## Spacing

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least the width of the arrow tip or the height of the "V" in the wordmark.

The icon and wordmark should not be compressed or crowded.

---

## Scale Guidance

### Small Use

- favicon
- browser tab
- app icon
- navigation bar icon

Use:

- symbol only
- circular badge if needed
- simplified monochrome form

### Medium Use

- product header
- website nav
- presentation title
- repository docs

Use:

- compact horizontal lockup
- standard horizontal lockup

### Large Use

- brand guidelines
- landing page hero
- keynote slides
- cover imagery

Use:

- full horizontal lockup
- large symbol as a graphic element
