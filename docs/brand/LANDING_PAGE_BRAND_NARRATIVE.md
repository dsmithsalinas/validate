## Hero Option 1

# Don't build the wrong thing.

Validate helps product teams decide whether an idea deserves engineering investment before they commit time, money, and focus.

## Supporting Copy

Most teams do not fail because they cannot build.

They fail because they build the wrong thing.

Validate evaluates customer evidence, product assumptions, decision risk, and strategic fit before producing a recommendation: Build, Validate, Pause, or Reject.

---

## Hero Option 2

# Turn evidence into product decisions.

Validate is an AI-powered product investment reasoning engine.

It helps teams separate facts, evidence, assumptions, and opinions so they can make better product decisions before engineering begins.

---

## Hero Option 3

# Product judgment, made explicit.

Validate does not generate PRDs first.

It asks better questions, evaluates evidence, surfaces assumptions, and helps teams decide whether an idea is worth building.

Documents come later.

Better decisions come first.

---

## Core Message

Validate helps teams answer one question:

> Should we invest time and money building this?

To answer that, Validate examines:

- customer evidence
- behavioral evidence
- business value
- strategic alignment
- confidence
- decision debt
- potential

---

## Product Framing

Validate is not:

- a PRD generator
- a chatbot
- a roadmap tool
- an AI writing assistant

Validate is:

- a product investment reasoning engine
- an evidence evaluation system
- a decision-quality tool
- a product judgment framework

---

## Short Description

Validate helps product teams evaluate whether an idea deserves investment by analyzing evidence, assumptions, confidence, decision debt, and potential.

---

## Feature Copy

### Evidence Classification

Separate facts, evidence, assumptions, and opinions before making a decision.

### Decision Debt

Understand the future cost of committing too early.

### Confidence

See how certain the recommendation is, and what would increase confidence.

### Potential

Recognize high-upside ideas without confusing possibility for proof.

### Recommendations

Receive one of four clear outcomes: Build, Validate, Pause, or Reject.

## Closing Section

# Build with evidence.

Validate helps teams slow down before expensive commitments, ask better questions, and make product decisions they can explain.

Because the best product work starts before the first line of code.
