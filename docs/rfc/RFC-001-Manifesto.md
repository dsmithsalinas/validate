## Mission

Help product teams make better investment decisions before they invest engineering time, money, and organizational focus.

## Vision

Create the world's most trusted reasoning engine for product investment decisions.

Not an AI that writes documents.

An AI that improves judgment.

## Why Validate Exists

Software has never been easier to build.

Artificial intelligence continues to reduce the cost of implementation.

As building becomes cheaper, deciding what deserves to be built becomes increasingly valuable.

Most failed products were not impossible to build.

They simply should not have been built.

Teams waste months building features customers never adopt.

Roadmaps become collections of requests instead of deliberate investments.

Opinions become strategy.

Confidence replaces evidence.

Validate exists to reverse that pattern.

## Core Beliefs

### Evidence is more valuable than opinions

The loudest customer is not necessarily representative.

The biggest prospect is not necessarily the market.

The founder is not always right.

Evidence wins.

### Uncertainty should never be hidden

If confidence is low, say so.

If evidence is weak, say so.

If assumptions dominate the recommendation, say so.

Trust comes from honesty.

### AI should challenge thinking, not reinforce it

Most AI assistants try to be agreeable.

Validate should be useful before it is agreeable.

The product should ask better questions, surface weak reasoning, and refuse unsupported recommendations.

### Documents are outputs, not the product

PRDs, user stories, roadmaps, and rollout plans can be useful.

But they are not the core value of Validate.

The product is the quality of the decision before those artifacts are created.

### Better decisions create better product leaders

Validate should not replace product judgment.

It should train it.

Every interaction should help the user become more evidence-aware, more honest about assumptions, and more disciplined about investment decisions.

## What Validate Is

Validate is an AI product investment reasoning engine.

It helps founders and product teams decide whether an opportunity deserves investment before engineering begins.

Validate acts more like an investment committee for engineering time than a writing assistant.

## What Validate Is Not

Validate is not:

- A PRD generator
- A Jira ticket generator
- A roadmap visualization tool
- A generic AI writing assistant
- A copilot that agrees with the user by default

Those may be interfaces or outputs.

They are not the product.

## Founding Product Question

Validate exists to answer:

> Should I invest time and money building this?

Everything else is secondary.

## Operating Principle

Every feature must increase decision quality.

If a feature does not help users make better product investment decisions, it does not belong in Validate yet.
